About Maxwell

Max supports individuals and couples in the sacred work of healing old wounds, exploring more satisfying and meaningful ways of being in the world, and coming into deeper awareness of our true nature. 

His work draws on long-term contemplative practice, a clinical background in somatic psychotherapy, shamanic ceremonial training, psychedelic therapy, and several bodywork modalities. Max began a daily meditation practice 14 years ago, and has studied and practiced extensively in Vajrayana and Zen Buddhist traditions, as well as Kashmir Shaivism and Advaita Vedanta. He received an MA in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from Naropa University, and a BA in Consciousness Studies from Goddard College. He has worked with earth medicines for nearly a decade and studied with multiple indigenous healing lineages, most intensively apprenticing in the Shipibo-Konibo tradition. He also has a professional background in craniosacral and massage therapy.

Max resides in the mountains outside of Boulder, Colorado and in his free time enjoys meditation, time with friends, far-ranging conversation, nature, music making, contact improv dance, and ceremonial prayer work.

Offerings

Therapeutic coaching to meet you where you are, and deep medicine work

One-on-One and Couples Sessions

Contemplative and somatically-oriented therapeutic coaching. We meet what is real in the relational field, settle into safety and trust, and home in on the natural unfolding that wants to happen in your life. This is presence-based work, meeting the details of your life story and circumstances, while making space to drop below the level of story into what is most real.

Medicine Work and Ceremonial Healing

Experiential healing in expanded states of awareness to open and heal our deepest wounds. This is work held with sacred reverence, care, and the attuned safety. We begin with several preparation sessions to develop clear intentions and ready the body-mind. The healing journey itself is held in a therapeutic or ceremonial style depending on client desire and orientation. We follow up with integration sessions to support the healing and insights landing fully to express in your day-to-day life.
